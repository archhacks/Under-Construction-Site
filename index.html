FAQ

Who can attend Local Hack Day?

Everyone is welcome to participate in Local Hack Day regardless of experience level, student status, or age. However, some Local Hack Day locations may have restrictions on who is able to participate at their specific site, which you can find on the event’s registration page. ArchHacks doesn’t! Everyone’s welcome!

What if I can’t code?

Everyone is welcome at Local Hack Day regardless of their coding experience. Many participants use Local Hack Day as an opportunity to learn some coding skills and network with other beginners. We also provide free technical workshops that you can attend to learn new skills.

Will there be swag, food, and prizes?

Yes, yes, and yes! We will feed you dinner and by attending you’ll have a chance to enter a raffle for a $75 gift card each night! It’s also an exclusive opportunity to pickup some awesome stickers and other surprises.
